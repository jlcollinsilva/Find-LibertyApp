Welcome to Go Liberty Game!

This is a Geocaching Game and your goal is to find the Status of Liberty monument.

You do not need any map, Go Liberty will guide you to the game destination.

Like the tradition Hot and Cold game you will received a different signal when you are walking away (Cold), getting close (Warm) and very very close (Hot).

You will know when you win (arrive) whe you received a Winner signal. So go to the Game Tab and play the game!!!!
